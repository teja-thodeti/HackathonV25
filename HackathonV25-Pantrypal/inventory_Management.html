Inventory Management

Track your ingredients, manage expiry dates, and avoid food waste efficiently.

Inventory Management Tricks

  • Barcode/RFID Technology: Use barcodes or RFID for real-time tracking.
  • Minimum/Maximum Stock Levels: Set reorder points to prevent stockouts.
  • Cycle Counts: Check small parts of inventory regularly instead of annual full counts.
  • Consolidate Orders: Combine orders to reduce costs and optimize stock levels.
  • ABC Analysis: Prioritize inventory based on product value and demand.
  • Drop Shipping: Ship products directly from suppliers to customers.
  • Demand Forecasting: Predict demand using data to avoid overstocking.
  • Cross-Docking: Ship products directly to customers from suppliers.
  • Stock Rotation (FIFO/LIFO): Rotate stock based on expiry or demand.
  • Inventory Software: Use cloud-based tracking and automation.
  • Clear Labeling/Organization: Label and organize inventory for efficiency.
  • Track Lead Times: Monitor supplier performance for timely stock arrivals.
  • Safety Stock: Keep backup stock for unexpected demand.
  • Review Policies: Regularly update inventory strategies.
  • Kanban System: Use visual cues to replenish stock.
  • Move Slow-Moving Items: Discount or bundle slow-selling products.
  • Negotiate Terms: Get flexible terms from suppliers for better stock control.
