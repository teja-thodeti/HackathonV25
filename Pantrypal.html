About Pantry Pal

PantryPal is designed to mitigate food waste by providing users with comprehensive inventory_Management tools. It offers features like meal planning, expiry notifications, and community sharing options, empowering users to make smarter food consumption choices. This platform aims to foster a community of users committed to reducing food waste and enhancing sustainability.
